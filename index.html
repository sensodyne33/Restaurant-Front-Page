Mission Statement

Louis' Sausage and Egg House has been in business for over 20 years! We specialize in Continental Style Breakfast and American Breakfast. We don't seek much from out customers. All we wish is for them to enjoy a wonderful warm meal to start out their day right. I hope you give us a chance and try out our long tested menu.
